5 Insights and a Visualization¶

I produced 5 insights and a visualization from the twitter_archive_master.csv dataset. I would take time here and look in detail what each insight mean.

Insight 1¶

Doggo dogs are the most retweeted¶

Some people retweet things they find insightful, entertaining and many more. A person might retweet a dog because he/she might find the dog attractive. The challenge with the dog_stage column is that a huge proportion of the dogs tweeted could not be classified. Nonetheless, working with the number of dogs which were able to be classified showed that doggo dogs were the most retweeted dogs followed closely by pupper, and then puppo and the last been floofer.
